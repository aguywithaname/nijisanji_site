Nijisanji Project

About

The Nijisanji Project is a digital influencer project that aims to advance the next generation of entertainment through various events, exclusive merchandise, and music production. Established in 2018 by Ichikara Inc., the group originally consisted of solely Japanese members, but has since expanded to other countries as an international project.

Currently, Nijisanji has over 170 active members worldwide, commonly referred to as “Virtual Livers”, who produce content on various video distribution platforms including YouTube, NicoNico Douga, and Bilibili.
