Top Members

This page is meant to highlight the members of Nijisanji. However, since there are currently over 170 members, it will be difficult to cover them all. Thus, is page will only focus on the 10 top members (in terms of YouTube subscribers) as of April 2021.

葛葉 / Kuzuha

Official description: A NEET vampire gamer who has been leaching off his parents and living with a pet pig who takes care of his belongings. He is whimsical, and carefree and has a habit of getting carried away. He is a constantly cash-strapped, causing him to have a strong love for money.

Personality and Streams: As mentioned before, he is a game addict and hopeless NEET, who also happens to be a vampire. As a VTuber, Kuzuha possesses an incredible capacity to entertain viewers due to his snappy comebacks and unexpected reactions. Kuzuha is very frank with members he's close with but is a bundle of anxiety in large gatherings. Excellent singer, excellent FPS player, very open with his desires (money and blood), and unabashedly cute at times.

月ノ美兎 / Tsukino Mito

Official description: A second-year high school student. She has a tsundere, seiso (wholesome) personality nature while trying to be a serious class president. Even though she does her best, she sometimes feels her efforts are in vain. She often wonders if she spoke too much after giving a speech and feels depressed afterwards.

Personality and Streams: A high school class rep with an interest in creative/unconventional humor. As one of the first to debut, her style helped shape Nijisanji and the Vtuber streaming scene as a whole. Regarded as “the face of Nijisanji”, she is highly respected amongst her peers and those in the industry, with many attributing her as the reason to why they became a VTuber. Her streams cover a wide variety of content from talking, games, music, and unique collabs/skits. She has a vast number of interests and experiences which makes her chatting streams (zatsudan) all the more enjoyable with her talent for storytelling.

叶 / Kanae

Official description: A young man with a healing presence (like that of an angel) and a fluffy, sweet way of speaking that can wash you away.

He looks like a student, but what he usually does is unknown.

Personality and Streams: A rather easily misunderstood gamer with (supposed) amnesia with a cat plush named Roto. He’s a prankster at heart and loves to tell pointless lies to trick people whenever he gets a chance. While he is best known for FPS games both niche and major, he dabbles in a wide variety of genres from simulation to MMORPGs. He has yet to go a full 24 hours without having streamed on at least one streaming platform ever since debuting.

本間ひまわり / Honma Hiwawari

Official description: A second-year high school student who loves games and cats. She began streaming her game playthroughs as a way to make friends. She is usually cheerful and a bit of an airhead, but when she plays games...?! For some reason, she always wears a sunflower on her head, and when she has a bad day, she uses it to read fortunes.

Personality and Streams: She is sunshine incarnate, bright and always positive, and often helps fellow colleagues off-stream. Despite being an airhead and mathematically inept, she is surprisingly skilled in Adobe After Effects and helps others create music videos for their own channels. After joining Nijisanji, she overcame her self-esteem issues and now shines with her strong personality and sense of individuality.

笹木咲 / Sasaki Saku

Official description: A second-year high school student from Kansai with a laid-back attitude and a love for video games and aquariums. Her favorite animals are pandas and Japanese pancake devilfish.

Personality and Streams: Although easy-going, she has a passion for games and is known for her intense competitive spirit; her talent for entertainment makes every second fun to watch. Close friend of Shiina Yuika, having known each other before joining Nijisanji.

鈴原るる / Suzuhara Lulu

Official description: A second-year girl who goes to an all-girls university. She's from a family of honorable origin and is a so-called "sheltered girl". To fix her ignorance of the world, she started streaming. She doesn't intend to be behind on trends and likes to try things out of curiosity. She likes singing and wants her voice to reach many people.

Personality and Streams: A quiet and calm girl who has an unexpectedly strong will and determination. A hardcore gamer who refuses to accept defeat. This, combined with her bizarre sayings, has earned her a fearsome reputation among fans and other Nijisanji members. Tends to stream frequently and for long periods of time, especially during weekends.

リゼ・ヘルエスタ / Lize Helesta

Official description: The second princess of the Helesta Kingdom. She is a top student in both literary and military arts. Her close friends describe her to be earnest, honest, trustworthy and reliable. As a qualified successor to the throne, she values daily training and communication with others.

Personality and Streams: The intelligent and articulate Second Princess of the Helesta Kingdom who delivers well-rounded streaming content. Has a knack in giving witty reactions to games and during collabs; she is also clever in picking up comments and giving humorous comebacks. Very knowledgeable, prim and proper, but often uses “dark society” jokes. She is entertaining even in collabs, showing different sides depending on her streaming partner. Interesting fact: Strangely enough, although perhaps fitting of her character, she is incredibly well versed with Japanese legal law and often shares her knowledge at unexpected times.

アンジュ・カトリーナ / Ange Katrina

Official description: She spends most of her time in a ramshackle hut studying alchemy. She is researching a way to turn her body into one that is more feminine.

Personality and Streams: She is a very animated Alchemist from the Helesta Kingdom with a lot of surprising (and cute) quirks to her personality. She is incredibly lively and engaging on stream but many of her peers describe her as shy and nervous in person. She often talks about her (usually unfortunate) past and personal life. Enjoys a wide variety of games, especially horror, and laid-back chats. Uses Nijisanji’s “Niji3D” tech regularly for both chat and game streams, delivering upbeat, entertaining reactions.

アルス・アルマル / Ars Almal

Official description: A novice wizard. The world of wizards seems to be a tough one, so she uses streaming as a way to relieve stress.

Personality and Streams: A shy witch who claims to come from a different dimension. Her cute bubbly pattern of speech is one of her charm points. Initially a shy person who had difficulty speaking in front of others, but lately has tried to improve that aspect of herself and is slowly becoming able to joke around with her colleagues. Recently, her mischievous side has started to show, much to the chagrin of her peers. Streams usually start late at night and can be quite long. In most games, she loves interacting with her viewers and although rare, English comments as well.

椎名唯華 / Shiina Yuika

Official description: A first-year high school student with a Kansai dialect who loves games. She is actually a psychic. She has a carefree and easy-going personality, usually unconcerned about her surroundings. Perhaps because of this, she is often possessed by various spirits.

Personality and Streams: Because she takes things at her own pace, her streams can either be relaxing or thrilling, depending on her mood at the time. Despite being a psychic, she is terrible with horror. Her communication skills are quite good, so collaborations streams are always refreshing and exciting. However, she can be quite mischievous and cunning. Close friends with Sasaki Saku as they knew each other before joining Nijisanji.

All information on this page is sourced from the offical Nijisanji website (translated into English) and Reddit page with slight modifications.
